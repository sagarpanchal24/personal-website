The world is entering an era where AI handles the obvious — the repetitive, the pattern-based, the routine. But physics doesn't bend to prompts. The hardest, most valuable problems are still grounded in first principles.

That's why I'm building a research-based consultancy — a firm that can tackle deep research in any domain. We start with what I know best: Solid Oxide Fuel Cells (SOFC) and predictive modelling of fuel cell lifetime.

Target launch: 2–3 years out.
